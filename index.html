CLOSE EYES

Fish fairy, is a kind of creature with fish head and human body, that inhabits the bottom of the sea. They have a pair of big eyes with no lids that cannot be closed.

In order to get along with her boyfriend, the protagonist tries her best to be what he likes—to close her eyes.

The store downstairs starts to sell goldfish. Among the goldfish sold in the store, the photographer sees a green fish, the same green as that girl’s hair.

PROCRASTINATION

The girl turns into a hamster with her cheeks stuffed with nuts every midnight. The number of nuts is proportional to the amount of unfinished homework the girl has done.

The girl will turn back into a human after eating the nuts and her homework will be automatically completed.

A MISSING EAR

While the ears were sitting on their owner's shoulder resting, the right ear accidentally fell off and separated from its owner.

The doctor tells the owner she has never seen this before. The wandering right ear also suffers various crises.

REPLACE

An old man produces robots and hopes to transform himself into a robot. His computer has been trying to hinder his plan, but the housework robot has been supporting the old man wholeheartedly.

The old man dies in the last step of his plan—replacing his head, and all of this has been the housework robot's plan.
